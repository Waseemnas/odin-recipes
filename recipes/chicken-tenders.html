Chicken Tenders

Description:

The chicken-tenders are first battered then baked.

Ingredients:

  • 1(9 ounce) chicken tenderloins
  • half cup finely crushed stuffing mix
  • 2 table spoons melted butter

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Line a 9x13 inch baking dish with foil. Rinse and pat dry the chicken and place in the baking dish.
  3. Combine the stuffing mix with the melted butter. Place stuffing mixture on top of the chicken tenders.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 40min.
